# TukTuk

Just a little mac app to help turn your sketchy sidecar into an even sketchier bus!!
[Why is this called TukTuk?](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Auto_rickshaw)

<img width="670" height="550" alt="TukTuk UI example" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/575972bc-2722-4d15-9cf6-18b0e06023ba" />

## Features:
* Automatically recognises Apple-Sidecar capable targets
* Allows registering these target to connect automatically when plugged in
* Can actually differentiate between these targets, unlike other apps I tried that will automatically connect whatever Sidecar device shows up first.
* Registers hotkeys to connect to your Sidecar devices quickly
* Is actually notarized AND will not persist in your dock for no reason
 
TukTuk is AI slop free, 100% guaranteed!!
